Few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. It has a diameter of about 12,742 kilometers (7,918 miles) and is home to a diverse range of ecosystems and species. The Earth's atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen, which supports life and protects the planet from harmful solar radiation. The planet's surface is covered by oceans, continents, and various landforms, making it a unique and dynamic environment.

Top 5 Facts about Earth

  • Earth is the only planet in our solar system that has liquid water on its surface.
  • The Earth's atmosphere protects us from meteoroids and harmful solar radiation.
  • Earth's rotation causes the cycle of day and night, while its revolution around the Sun creates the seasons.
  • Earth has a natural satellite, the Moon, which influences tides and stabilizes the planet's axial tilt.
  • Earth is home to millions of species of plants and animals, making it a biodiversity hotspot.
